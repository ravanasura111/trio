Maintenance Engineer Job Description

As a Maintenance Engineer, you will be required to install new equipment, conduct routine maintenance, and troubleshoot equipment faults on site. You may also be required to remove old equipment and train staff on equipment operating procedures. To ensure success as a Maintenance Engineer, you should have extensive knowledge of industrial equipment, the ability to work well under pressure, and demonstrate high-level problem-solving skills. An outstanding Maintenance Engineer should be able to service, troubleshoot, and repair equipment without causing extended equipment downtime.


Maintenance Engineer Requirements:

Knowledge of EPA and OSHA guidelines.
Advanced knowledge of mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and heating systems.
Ability to read system schematics.
Advanced troubleshooting skills.
Understanding of safety and equipment procedures.
Good communication and training skills.
Ability to work with heavy equipment.
