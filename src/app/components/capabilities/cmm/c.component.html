Coordinate Measuring Machine

TrioVision uses one of the biggest portable arm in current industry 4.5 meter circular reach. All patterns and molds as per customer requirements will undergo CMM inspections to ensure the accuracies are maintained. Any patterns bigger in size than 20 square meter are done using laser tracker from our channel partners. ​

Laser scanning for inspection or reverse engineering can also be performed depending on the customer’s requirements. Whether it is cad model comparison or dimensional verifications it is possible to submit reporters according to the needs.
