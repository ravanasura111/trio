Kolkata Metro
TrioVision has supplied interior parts to Indian Railways for its ICF factory in Chennai. Fire standard followed is NPP 502. Number of train sets produced is 10 with a gelcoat finish and gloss upto 80 +/- 10. This is one of the metro coach that is done by ICF on its own with latest standards and specifications in place.
