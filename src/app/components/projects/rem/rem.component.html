Montreal REM

TrioVision in collaboration with RPC Technologies is executing Metro project for Montreal. The train is built by Alstom Transportation at its Sri City facility and exported to Canada. With a total trains of 106 having 2 coaches per train this is the second export project of Alstom Transportation. The scopes include Front End, Cab Fitting, Driver’s Desk, Electrical Cubicles and Door Pillars.

Made to meet stringent fire standards of EN 45545 HL 2, and painted with a 3 coat system from Bhimrajka Mader and with a great combination of colours this is truly a “Made in India” product. Despite pandemic situation, TrioVision/RPC/Alstom have been relentlessly working on this project to ramp up and meet desired timelines.
