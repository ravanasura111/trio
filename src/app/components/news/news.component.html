1.IIT Tirupati Visiting

Mr. Subba Reddy Daggumati
Assistant Professor, Mechanical Engineering from IIT Tirupati

Date:25th December 2020

Mr. Subba Reddy Daggumati, Assistant Professor, Mechanical Engineering from IIT Tirupati has visited TrioVision facility to understand the products and processes that we are using. From an extensive research background in Advanced Composites, Mr. Subba Reddy was very much impressed and excited for a joint Univeristy/Industry collaboration to take forward some exciting Research projects forward. IIT Tirupati also has state of the art structural testing facilities which will support TrioVision to jointly move some advance material research formulations forward.

Expantion Plans of Triovision

TrioVision is happy to announce that it is entering into a phase of expansion looking at the tremendous growth of Railways. The new facility is planned in a area of 90,000 square meter which will have a state of the art dedicated Rail and Wind production. Along with the production lines, Aerospace and Defence production line is also planned in the new facility. TrioVision also is planning to have its own fabrication facility which can not only provide metal supply to its own Rail and Wind requirement but also could support other companies. TrioVision is also in talks to have an innovation center for Electric Vehicle Development. With the expansion TrioVision expects to further give employment upto 1,000 once all facilities start operating in their full capacity.
