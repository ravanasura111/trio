Lush Motors
Smart SUMV For Lush Motors
TrioVision in collaboration with Lush Motors has designed a Smart Urban Mobility Vehicle- 2 seater x 3 wheeler for city commuting. The vehicle is built in GFRP using closed molding process with specialized loose insert tooling.
Traffic Booth
Police Traffic Booth
TrioVision in collaboration with Gaha Innofab has designed and developed from concept 6.5 feet and 4.5 feet circular police traffic booths supplied to Signpost in Bengaluru. These booths have been of great benefit to the BBMP policemen where they can sit inside and still have 360 degrees viewing along with signal controls and CCTV controlled from the booth.
WindStrip
TrioVision in collaboration with Windistrip LLC based at Minnesota
The 1290 series that is developed has 18 blades with a baseline of Savonius effect and patented design by Windstrip. The blades are designed in such a way that the wind cutting direction is carefully simulated allowing the system to start running at barely 2 meter per second wind speeds.
Robotic Milling
CNC Machining
TrioVision uses one of the biggest robotic milling machine in India with 8 axis and can mill upto 15 meter long x 5 meter wide x 3 meter height in single piece. Patterns bigger than this normally gets machined in splits and can be joined accurately with our skilled manpower.
